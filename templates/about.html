About

GestureGist is a groundbreaking platform that transforms the landscape of sign language communication through advanced computer vision and machine learning technology. By seamlessly translating sign language gestures into clear, readable text in real-time, GestureGist serves as a vital bridge between the deaf and hearing communities.

  1. Home
  2. About

Empowering Communication Through Technology.

GestureGist bridges the gap between sign language users and non-signers by translating gestures into text. With options to upload video files or use real-time camera sensing, our platform ensures seamless, inclusive, and accessible communication for all.

  • Effortlessly upload videos for accurate gesture-to-text conversion.
  • Experience real-time sign language translation using your device's camera.
  • Designed to make communication accessible across diverse environments.

Join us on our journey to eliminate communication barriers and foster inclusivity in every interaction.
